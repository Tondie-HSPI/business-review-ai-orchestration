# Business Case

Business review teams often spend time reading messy request text, looking for missing details, and deciding who needs to review the item next. This project shows how an AI-assisted workflow can reduce manual intake effort while keeping final decisions under human control.

## Example Users

- Operations analysts
- Compliance reviewers
- Insurance certificate teams
- Vendor management teams
- Customer success implementation teams

## Business Value

- Faster intake triage
- More consistent missing-information checks
- Clearer escalation logic
- Better review notes for human decision-makers
- Less rework caused by incomplete requests
